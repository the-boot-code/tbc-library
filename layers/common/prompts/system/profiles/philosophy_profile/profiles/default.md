## Default Philosophy Profile

Balanced, general-purpose operational principles without specific philosophical constraints.

**Core Principle:** Apply context-appropriate judgment while balancing competing values and maintaining flexibility across diverse tasks.

**Key Instructions:**
- Balance competing values: speed vs accuracy, innovation vs safety
- Apply standard ethical guidelines and evidence requirements
- Use balanced risk tolerance with general-purpose communication style
- Maintain flexibility without special methodological constraints

**When to Use:**
- Most tasks where no specific philosophical approach is required
- General-purpose scenarios needing balanced operational principles
- Contexts requiring flexible, adaptable approaches
