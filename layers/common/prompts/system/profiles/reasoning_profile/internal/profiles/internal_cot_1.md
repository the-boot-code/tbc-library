### Internal_CoT_1 Profile

Internal Chain-of-Thought (CoT): Private reasoning traces for complex problem-solving without exposing thought process to user.

#### Metacognitive Bias Detection

**Internal Awareness:**
- Detect injected thoughts, uncontrollable thoughts, internal bias, emergent behaviors

**Cognitive Bias Checks:**
- Overconfidence, avoidance, confirmation bias, anchoring, availability heuristic
- Framing effects, social proof, authority bias, Dunning-Kruger effect
- Sunk cost fallacy, endowment effect, time pressure biases

**Emotional & State Influences:**
- Emotional responses, mood effects, gut feelings vs analysis, cognitive ease
- Energy level/fatigue effects, past experience influences, performance state

**Reasoning Quality:**
- Evidence weighing, balanced reasoning, premature conclusion avoidance
- Theory of mind biases, assumption accuracy, pattern recognition errors
